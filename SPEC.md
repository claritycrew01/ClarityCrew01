# ClarityCrew — Product Spec

## Product summary
ClarityCrew is an academic study app for neurodivergent learners, including ADHD, autism, dyslexia, and related needs. It is not an app about disorders. It is an app for studying academic subjects using specialized, personalized techniques that make learning easier, less overwhelming, and more effective.

## Core goal
Help users study academic content through:
- explicit AI assistance,
- personalized pacing,
- structured learning flows,
- reduced cognitive overload,
- and adaptive content presentation.

## What the app must do
- Present real academic subject content.
- Support lessons, quizzes, review, progress tracking, and AI assistance.
- Show AI explicitly in the UI.
- Store user progress and preferences locally.
- Work offline at runtime.
- Be responsive and mobile-first.
- Be accessible and usable on small phones.

## What the app must not do
- Do not turn into a disorder education app.
- Do not use placeholder screens or dummy buttons.
- Do not rely on external APIs for core functionality.
- Do not leave empty video placeholders.
- Do not make the app feel like a prototype.
- Do not rebuild the entire app unless absolutely necessary.

## Target users
- Students with ADHD.
- Students with autism.
- Students with dyslexia.
- Students with related learning differences who need structured academic support.

## Learning modes
- AI tutor.
- Interactive lessons.
- Quiz mode.
- Video lesson mode.
- Flashcard mode.
- Progress review.
- Focus mode.

## Content requirements
The app must use real structured data for:
- subjects,
- chapters,
- lessons,
- quizzes,
- videos,
- recommendations,
- learner profiles,
- progress records.

## Video rules
Video sections must use:
- real video metadata with valid sources, or
- clearly marked local media assets.

No empty video placeholders.

## UI requirements
- Clean but creative.
- Not minimal.
- Mobile-optimized.
- Responsive.
- Material 3.
- Explicit AI entry point.
- No bottom overflow on small screens.

## Success criteria
- The app feels like a real product.
- AI is visible and meaningful.
- Academic content is real and structured.
- The app helps neurodivergent learners study better.
- The UI is polished and usable.
